Isa's Dance Life!

My Dance Background

I have been dancing since she was 5 years old. I started at a small dance studio in Lower Manhattan called the Lower East Side Dance Academy. Here, I learned Ballet, Jazz, and Tap, and later on joined the studios competition team.

I left my elementary school in 3rd grade to attend Ballet Tech, a NYC public school for dance, which is like LaGuardia, only much smaller and solely for dance. I continued to train in Classical Ballet and learned the repertory of the school's founder, Eliot Feld, to perform in an end-of-year performance called "Kids Dance", which was performed at the Joyce Theater in Manhattan, New York.

Present Day

I have been dancing at LaGuardia High School since my freshman year.

At LaGuardia, I have been exposed to the Modern Techniques of Graham, Limon, and Horton, and Contemporary dance. I have continued taking classes in Ballet, Tap, and Jazz. In my Junior Year, I was able to choreograph a piece alongside 4 other dance majors to perform at a showcase in the Little Flower Theater.
